ABOUT  US

Rhine Exports Private Limited


It is a company formed in the year 1996 by an Ex-Marine engineer, Mr. Jasbir Singh with an aim to help the marine sector trouble shoot their problems.

We are proud that we have achieved our aim and with time our aim gets more firm and stronger.

We stand on values of Trust, Sincerity, Quality, Hard work and believe in fair business practices. We are leading merchant exporters of all new & reconditioned marine machinery. Our company is an ISO 9001-2008 certified, meeting all the required standards.
